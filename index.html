Workshop on Best Practices for HPC Training @SC14

Date: Friday, 21 November, 2014

Deadline for full paper submission of the talks: January 15st, 2015

Workshop Format

The workshop will be a mix of short (10-15minute) contributed presentations and an open discussion in which we will develop a workshop report. Presentations should be submitted to the linnk the organisers have sent you. This github space will be used on future collaboration events.

Contact us

Please use the attached form to sign for further information on this and next issues of the Workshop.
